# Code of Conduct

**Read in:** [English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [Русский](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.ru.md)


This project follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## In short

- Be respectful, technical, and concise.
- No personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination.
- Focus on the code and its effects, not on the person.
- Disagreements about technical direction are welcome; ad-hominem is not.

## Reporting

Email the maintainer at the address in `pyproject.toml`. Reports are
confidential. Maintainers may take action including warning, temporary
ban, or permanent ban.
